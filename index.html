About

The Count of Monte Cristo is a generational favorite in my family. They showed me this movie when I was younger, and it’s been my favorite ever since. The plot, acting, and character development are all excellent. The theme of revenge and redemption keeps you hooked from start to finish.

Fun Facts

  • The prison Edmond was sent to was a real fortress located on the Île d'If near Marseille, France.
  • Jim Caviezel (Edmond) learned fencing and horseback riding for his role.
