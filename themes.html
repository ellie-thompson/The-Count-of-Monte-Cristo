Revenge

Edmond’s journey is driven by a desire for revenge — but as he executes his plan, he realizes vengeance comes with a heavy cost.

“How did I escape? With difficulty. How did I plan this moment? With pleasure.”

Redemption

Through forgiveness and self-reflection, Edmond learns that true freedom comes not from revenge, but from letting go of hatred.

“Until the day God will deign to reveal the future to man, all human wisdom is contained in these two words—Wait and Hope.”
