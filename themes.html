Revenge

Edmond’s transformation into the Count is fueled by vengeance. The film explores how revenge can consume a person and reshape their identity.

“How did I escape? With difficulty. How did I plan this moment? With pleasure.”

Redemption

As Edmond carries out his plan, he confronts the emotional cost of revenge and discovers that forgiveness may lead to greater peace.

“Until the day God will deign to reveal the future to man, all human wisdom is contained in these two words—Wait and Hope.”
