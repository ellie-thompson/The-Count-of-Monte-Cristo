Edmond Dantès / The Count of Monte Cristo

Once a kind and loyal sailor, Edmond is betrayed and imprisoned unjustly. After escaping, he reinvents himself as the wealthy Count of Monte Cristo to seek vengeance on those who wronged him.

Fernand Mondego

Edmond’s treacherous friend who betrays him out of jealousy. He rises to wealth and power but ultimately faces the consequences of his deceit.

Mercedes

Edmond’s former love, torn between loyalty and survival. Her story adds emotional depth and tragedy to the Count’s revenge.
